# Data Connect

[Data Connect](https://www.sheffield.ac.uk/data-connect) is a University of Sheffield Centre of Excellence aiming to improve researcher access to health and social care data enhancing research capability and driving innovation. 

## Vision
* A trusted partner facilitating research using health and social care data, to improve lives regionally, nationally and globally.
* An exemplary support service for research, that simplifies data access, ensures compliance and delivers research-ready data to drive discovery and innovation.

## Featured Projects
*   [BHF-SmartHealth](https://github.com/Data-Connect-UoS/BHF-SmartHealth) - A research project focused on collecting and linking smartphone and wearable device data with traditional health records via the Secure Data Environment (SDE) for Yorkshire and the Humber.
*   [CUREd+](https://sheffield.ac.uk/data-connect/data-assets/cured-research-database) - the Centre for Urgent and Emergency Care Research Database (CUREd+) is a secure health research database used to to track patient journeys through 111, ambulance services, and emergency departments across England.
*   [ConSY](https://sheffield.ac.uk/data-connect/data-assets/connected-south-yorkshire-consy-data-asset) - a secure regional database co-produced by Data Connect service and the South Yorkshire Integrated Care Board to link healthcare records for research that improves patient outcomes.
